Asia on Plate - Customer Support Guide

ABOUT US
--------
Asia on Plate is Berlin's premier online Asian grocery store, established in 2020. We deliver authentic Asian products to your doorstep across Berlin and surrounding areas. Our mission is to bring the authentic taste of Asia to German households with fresh, high-quality ingredients sourced directly from trusted suppliers across Asia.

We specialize in products from Japan, Korea, China, Thailand, India, Vietnam, Philippines, and Indonesia. Our warehouse stocks over 2,000 different products, from everyday essentials to specialty items that are hard to find elsewhere in Berlin.

DELIVERY INFORMATION
-------------------
Q: Which areas do you deliver to?
A: We deliver to all Berlin districts including:
   - Central: Mitte, Tiergarten, Wedding
   - East: Friedrichshain, Kreuzberg, Lichtenberg, Marzahn-Hellersdorf
   - North: Pankow, Prenzlauer Berg, Reinickendorf
   - South: Neukölln, Tempelhof-Schöneberg, Steglitz-Zehlendorf
   - West: Charlottenburg-Wilmersdorf, Spandau
   We also deliver to Potsdam and selected areas in Brandenburg (additional €2.99 fee)

Q: How long does delivery take?
A: - Standard delivery: 1-2 business days
   - Express delivery: Same day (orders before 12 PM, €7.99 extra)
   - Scheduled delivery: Choose your preferred time slot (€2.99 extra)
   - Weekend delivery: Saturday only, orders before Friday 6 PM

Q: What are delivery costs?
A: - Orders under €39: €4.99 delivery fee
   - Orders €39 and above: FREE standard delivery
   - Express same-day delivery: €7.99 (regardless of order value)
   - Weekend delivery: €6.99 extra
   - Brandenburg/Potsdam: €2.99 extra

Q: When do you deliver?
A: Monday to Saturday, 9 AM - 8 PM. No Sunday deliveries.
   Express delivery available Monday-Friday until 6 PM.
   You can track your delivery in real-time via our app or website.

Q: What if I'm not home during delivery?
A: Our drivers will attempt delivery twice. If unsuccessful, packages are left at a nearby pickup point (DHL Packstation or local shop). You'll receive SMS notification with pickup details.

ORDER TRACKING & STATUS
----------------------
Q: How can I track my order?
A: You can track your order through:
   - Our website: Login to your account and check "My Orders"
   - Email: You'll receive tracking number via email
   - SMS: Real-time updates sent to your phone
   - App: Download our mobile app for instant notifications

Q: What do the order statuses mean?
A: - "Order Received": We've received your order and payment
   - "Processing": Items are being picked and packed
   - "Ready for Dispatch": Order is packed and waiting for pickup
   - "Out for Delivery": Driver is on the way to your address
   - "Delivered": Order successfully delivered
   - "Failed Delivery": Delivery attempt unsuccessful

RETURNS & REFUNDS
-----------------
Q: What is your return policy?
A: - Non-perishable items: 14 days from delivery date
   - Perishable items: Only if damaged, expired, or incorrect item
   - Unopened items in original packaging only
   - Items must be in sellable condition

Q: How do I return an item?
A: 1. Contact us within 14 days via email or phone
   2. Provide order number and reason for return
   3. We'll send you a prepaid return label
   4. Pack items securely and attach the label
   5. Drop off at any DHL location

Q: When will I get my refund?
A: - Refunds processed within 3-5 business days after we receive returned items
   - Money returned to original payment method
   - PayPal refunds: 1-2 business days
   - Credit card refunds: 3-7 business days
   - Bank transfer refunds: 5-7 business days

Q: What if my product is damaged or expired?
A: We'll immediately replace it or provide a full refund. Please:
   - Take photos of the damaged/expired product
   - Contact us within 24 hours of delivery
   - Keep the product until we advise disposal
   - We may arrange immediate pickup of damaged goods

Q: Can I exchange an item?
A: Yes, exchanges are possible for:
   - Wrong size/variant of the same product
   - Different flavor of the same brand
   - Similar product of equal or lesser value
   Contact us to arrange an exchange.

TROUBLESHOOTING & FAQ
--------------------
Q: My order hasn't arrived, what should I do?
A: 1. Check your tracking information first
   2. Contact our delivery partner if out for delivery
   3. Call our emergency delivery line: +49 176 9876 5432
   4. We'll investigate and provide updates within 2 hours

Q: I received the wrong item, what now?
A: 1. Don't worry, mistakes happen!
   2. Take a photo of what you received
   3. Contact us immediately with your order number
   4. We'll arrange pickup of wrong item and send correct one
   5. You keep any perishable wrong items as compensation

Q: Can I modify my order after placing it?
A: - If order status is "Order Received": Yes, contact us immediately
   - If status is "Processing": Limited changes possible
   - If "Ready for Dispatch" or later: No changes possible

Q: Do you offer gift cards?
A: Yes! Digital gift cards available in €25, €50, €100 denominations.
   Perfect for food lovers and can be used for any products.

Q: Can I pre-order items that are out of stock?
A: Yes, you can join the waitlist for out-of-stock items.
   We'll notify you when they're back and reserve them for 48 hours.
